About

In 2020 I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Pubic Affairs from Indiana University. This was a time where I still was a bit unsure of where I wanted to take my career. After thinking back to the design and web dev classes I had enjoyed at IU and doing some research, I came across Eleven Fifty Academy and enrolled in their web development course. This is where I learned the bulk of my coding skills through all of the projects you see on this porfolio which was also done during this class. Upon earning my certification I landed an RPA job with a company called RoboSource.

I found the work to be extremely rewarding from seeing what automation can do to help all kinds of companies in different industries. One project I worked on was creating email templates in HTML and CSS for RoboSource emails and this sparked my interest in Digital Marketing. It was something that I had previosly thought of doing before Eleven Fifty Academy, but after actually doing work related to the field I have started to shift gears to this. Once my contract with RoboSource was done I took a position to work with the marketing team at The Bulldog Bar and Lounge.

While working with them, I have been able to get a sample of different tasks in marketing from sending out email campaigns, graphic design for content, and web site management systems to name a few. I have also taken some free time to look into SEO and SEM and got certifications in Fundamentals to Digital Marketing Course and Google Search Ads. The skills that I have learned over the past couple of years have given me a great foundation for a career in Digital Marketing. Please feel free to check out my resume and connect with me on LinkedIn!
